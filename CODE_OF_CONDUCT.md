# Code of Conduct

This project follows the Contributor Covenant v2.1. We expect all contributors and maintainers to adhere to the following code of conduct to ensure a friendly and productive community.

## Our Pledge

In the interest of fostering an open and welcoming environment, we as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in our project and community a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and considerate in all interactions.
- Use welcoming and inclusive language.
- Be patient and assume good intent.
- Provide constructive feedback and be open to feedback on your own contributions.

## Unacceptable Behavior

Examples of unacceptable behavior include, but are not limited to:

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination
- Abusive or threatening language
- Publication of private information without consent
- Any behavior that a reasonable person would consider inappropriate in a professional setting

## Reporting Guidelines

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it via email to the project maintainers (primary contact: Joseph Jonathan Fernandes). Include the following:
- A description of the incident
- Screenshots or logs if available
- The time and date of the incident
- Identities of the people involved (if known)

Reports will be handled confidentially and promptly. Maintainers will take appropriate action based on the severity of the report.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for enforcing the code of conduct. Consequences for unacceptable behavior may include warnings, temporary or permanent ban from contributing, or escalation to platform providers.
